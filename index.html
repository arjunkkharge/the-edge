⚡ Who I Am as a Trader

I am a disciplined ICT trader on a $50,000 prop firm challenge. I trade MNQ, MES, and MGC across the Asia, London, NY, and London Close sessions.

My entry model: liquidity sweeps of ERL/IRL levels (daily/weekly/session highs & lows, swing points) + candle behaviour confirmation + lower timeframe entry via BOS, FVG, IFVG, or swing failure / 2nd stage FVG delivery model.

My one rule that cannot be broken: after any loss, I close the charts immediately. No revenge trading. No exceptions.

🎯 Entry Process — Step by Step

1

HTF Bias (Daily / 4H)

Identify the draw on liquidity. Are we IRL→ERL or ERL→IRL? Mark key levels: daily/weekly highs & lows, session highs & lows. These co-incide with swing points — this is your map for the session.

2

Wait for the Killzone

Do NOT look for entries before your session opens. London 2AM NY, Asia 8PM NY, NY open 8AM, London Close 10AM. Patience here is your edge. Price before killzone = noise.

3

Liquidity Sweep

Wait for price to sweep an ERL or IRL — daily/weekly high or low, session high or low, or a clear swing point (SSL or BSL taken). No sweep = no trade. The sweep is the trigger.

4

Candle Behaviour After Sweep

Watch the candle close after the sweep. Does it wick out and reject back inside? Strong wick + close back inside the range = sweep was real. If candle keeps going beyond the level = wait or skip.

5

Drop to M5 / M2 / M1

Go to lower timeframe. Look for a Break of Structure (BOS), FVG, or IFVG. For swing failure + continuation model: confirm 2nd stage FVG delivery on LTF. This is your precise entry signal.

6

Size & Enter

Use the Calculator page to get exact contract count. Entry at FVG/IFVG/BOS. Stop below/above the sweep. TP at opposing liquidity. Minimum 1:2 R:R — no exceptions.

7

Log Immediately After Close

Every single trade, win or loss. All fields. No skipping. This journal is how you improve and find your edge. One day the data will tell you something your gut didn't.

📋 Rules

Always Do

  • Wait for killzone before any entry
  • Confirm HTF draw on liquidity first
  • Wait for a confirmed sweep
  • Watch candle behaviour after sweep
  • Find entry model on M5/M2/M1
  • Calculate size before entering
  • Minimum 1:2 R:R at all times
  • Log every trade immediately after
  • Review journal every Sunday

Never Do

  • NEVER trade after a loss today
  • NEVER take more than 3 trades/day
  • NEVER enter outside a killzone
  • NEVER enter without a sweep
  • NEVER move SL closer to price
  • NEVER FOMO or boredom trade
  • NEVER skip logging a trade
  • NEVER size up after a loss
  • NEVER trade without a plan

🛑 After a Loss — Exact Protocol

1

Close charts immediately

Not in 5 minutes. Right now. Alt+F4 if needed.

2

Log the trade honestly

Did you follow all rules? Be completely honest. Write it down.

3

Step away for 30 minutes

Walk, drink water, breathe. Fully reset your state.

4

Do not open charts again that session

One −$100 loss is nothing. A revenge spiral turning it into −$300 ends your challenge. One good triple-win day ($600) wipes out 6 loss days. Protect the account above all else.
